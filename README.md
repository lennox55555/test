1. Design Process Crash Course

>In this project, I conducted an interview with an individual to gain insights into their daily tasks and identified their latent needs. Using this information, I designed a product tailored to their specific requirements. Through the interview process, I uncovered crucial details about their routine and preferences, which enabled me to develop a solution that exceeded their expectations. By combining user-centered design principles with empathetic research, I successfully addressed their unknown needs and created a product that will greatly enhance their daily tasks.
